Why We
Exist

Wellness became a chore. A 5 AM cold plunge, a bitter green juice, a clinical spreadsheet of "optimization." Somewhere along the way, we forgot that the point of being alive is to actually feel something good.

"We are the antidote to the optimized life. We are the pulse of the late night. We are the permission you didn't know you needed."

Vibe Check was born in a basement, fueled by caffeine and a refusal to settle for "fine." We normalize self-care by making it rebellious, messy, and loud.
